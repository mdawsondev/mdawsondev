About

About Me

Prior to web development, I worked as an artist, writer, and amateur programmer. After freelancing for a while, I decided I wanted more stability in my work.

I've built websites as a hobby since I was young, and the more I looked into career opportunities, the more I fell in love with development. After working on a couple of larger projects, I knew web development was the career I wanted.

About This Site

Languages: HTML, Sass, ES6

Libraries: Animate.css, Font Awesome, lazyload, Normalize.css, particles.js, Smooth Scroll

Tech: VSCode, git, GitHub, webpack, yarn

Theory: BEM, Modular Programming, Mobile-First Design

This site was rebuilt from the ground up and represents the second major version! It was written using a variety of skills, elements, and technology to show my understanding of development.

Sections are compiled using different CSS techniques, coupled with modular design and BEM naming. The site is designed fully responsive, and mobile friendly.
